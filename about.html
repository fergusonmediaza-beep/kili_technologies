About Us

Kili Tech is a renewable-energy technology company dedicated to accelerating Africa’s transition to clean, reliable, and financially sustainable power. We specialise in building advanced Data Acquisition Systems (DAS) and Energy Management Systems (EMS) that enable organisations to capture, verify, optimise, and monetise their energy data with confidence.

Rooted in innovation and supported by strategic advisors, industry experts, and a strong technological backbone, Kili Tech delivers end-to-end visibility, operational efficiency, and new revenue pathways for organisations investing in renewable energy.
