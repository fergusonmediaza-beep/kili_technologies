Why Africa?

Africa is home to 60% of the world's best solar resources and vast untapped wind, hydro, and geothermal potential—yet it accounts for less than 3% of global cumulative CO₂ emissions. Despite this, over 600 million people in Africa still lack access to electricity, and many businesses face crippling costs from unreliable grids and diesel dependence.

But this isn't just a challenge—it's the greatest clean energy opportunity of our time.

At Kili Tech, we see a continent poised for leapfrog innovation. With falling renewable costs, rising digital infrastructure, and a young, tech-savvy population, Africa doesn't need to replicate the fossil-fueled past—it can build a decentralized, data-driven, and democratized energy future from the ground up.
