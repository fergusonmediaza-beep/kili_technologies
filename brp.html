BRP-as-a-Service, Bank-Backed Energy Reliability & Revenue

Turnkey BRP(BalancingResponsibleParty) service for generators entering SAWE—supported by a Bank’s liquidity and settlement guarantees.

Enable independent power producers (IPPs) and embedded generators to comply with SAWEM balance responsibility requirements without in-house capabilities.

Day-ahead & intraday forecasting, imbalance management, settlement, and market interfacing with NTCSA.

Provides settlement accounts, intraday liquidity, and performance guarantees to back BRP collateral obligations.

Simplifies compliance, lowers financial barriers, and converts operational risk into a service fee model.

Operating Flow

Generator signs BRP-as-a-Service SLA (via DAS/EMS).

Forecasting engine submits DA/ID schedules to SAWEM.

Bank ensures liquidity and collateral compliance standard adherence

Settlements executed through bank-managed accounts (T+0/T+1)
